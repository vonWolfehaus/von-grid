Instructions

  • Left Click: create cell
  • Shift + Left Click: paint cells
  • Right Click: remove cell
  • Shift + Right Click: erase cells
  • Mousewheel: adjust zoom of camera
  • Shift+Mousewheel: adjust height of cell
  • Left click and drag: orbit camera
  • Right click and drag: pan camera

Editor settings

Map size: 20 Height step: 3
